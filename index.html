How to Play

Kid Cakes Maker Cooking Bakery is an exciting casual cooking simulation game where you bake delicious cakes with cute animals. Shop for ingredients, mix them perfectly, and create beautiful cake designs. Ready to become the ultimate pastry chef?

Controls

  • Shop for ingredients and cooking tools
  • Mix ingredients in bowls for perfect recipes
  • Put mixtures in moulds and bake in oven

Tips & Tricks

  • Choose the right ingredients for each recipe
  • Time your baking perfectly for best results
  • The more creative your decoration, the higher your score!
